The Analogy

Think about Pandora.

Their intellectual property isn't the music—it's the Music Genome Project: a system of definitions encoded as data. Those definitions feed algorithms that power recommendation and advertising. The music is the surface. The genome is the system.

What I've built operates similarly. We transform raw, live data streams into mathematical models. Those models become the foundation for systems of gaming and media.

One intelligence layer. Multiple experience systems. Each feeding on the same underlying reality.

What This Enables

Participation and narrative emerge from the same trigger, at the same moment, for the same reason.

This is what I mean by designing systems, not features. The intelligence layer that powers games and media didn't exist before I proposed it. I brought the architecture; now it's become central to the product.
